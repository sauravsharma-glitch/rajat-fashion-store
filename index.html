Our Story

Built by a tailor's daughter who refused to compromise.

Urban Threads began in a single room above a fabric market, where founder Maya Lindqvist hand-cut her first capsule collection because nothing on the high street felt deliberate enough. A decade later, that same obsession with cut, cloth, and detail runs through every piece we make.

Our mission is simple: make premium fashion accessible to people who refuse to blend in. Our vision is a wardrobe built on fewer, better things — garments that earn their place season after season.
